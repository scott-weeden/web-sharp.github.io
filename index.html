Public Notice

We regret to inform our customers that due to a recent court ruling in a dispute over accounting practices with BSI Financial, Web Sharp Studios will be forced to freeze accounts for 90 days, while entering Chapter 13 bankruptcy protection.

During the appeals process we intend to act with transparency and timeliness and apologize for the undue expense and costs associated with this process pursuant to the Bankruptcy Abuse Prevention and Consumer Protection Act of 2011.

This notice will expire February 15th 2024
